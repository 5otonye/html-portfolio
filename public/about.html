About Me

Born in the analog age on a crisp autumn morning in the bustling city of the Northern and Easter Hemisphere somewhere in the globe, i am the first child of Stella and Ibiwari, a hardworking couple who instilled in their children the values of kindness, honesty, and integrity.

Raised by parents who encouraged my curiosity and thirst for knowledge, from an early age i was captivated by the power of technology and the endless possibilities it presented. This influenced my educational background and my passion for science and technology as well as coding only grew stronger as I delved deeper into the intricacies of computer science. Upon graduating from college, I embarked on a career as a programmer, joining a startup company that would revolutionizing the way people interacted with technology.

Growing through the ranks I would want to be a valuable asset to the world of programming, contributing innovative ideas with a legacy that extends far beyond lines of code to serve as an inspiration to aspiring programmers everywhere, proving that with passion, perseverance, and dedication, anything is possible to achieve.
